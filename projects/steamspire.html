SteamSpire Chronicles

Overview - Team Project

Hack n’ Dash is a top-down roguelike with emphasis on fluid, action-packed combat. In this game, you’ll be skillfully dashing your way through a clockwork dungeon and using your momentum to your advantage, throwing your weight against enemies and knocking your foes around with style.

Every playthrough offers a fresh challenge thanks to procedurally generated levels and randomized item distribution. Players must adapt on the fly, learning from each run as they face unpredictable environments and ever-changing combat scenarios.
